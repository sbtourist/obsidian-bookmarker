Obsidian Bookmarker

Turn Obsidian into your bookmark manager

This is an unofficial extension for Obsidian that allows you to easily bookmark a webpage and clip a webpage selection to a note in Obsidian.

Here you can configure the extension and test the configuration.

Use {date}, {datetime}, {year}, {month}, {day}, {time}, {title} and {zettel} as placeholders
Use {title} and {link} as placeholders
Use {clip}, {date}, {datetime}, {year}, {month}, {day}, {time}, {title}, {zettel}, {og:image} and {url} as placeholders
